I oft find myself drawing inward
Even as I try to escape myself
There's a strange sort of peace in solitude,
Whether I find peace is a different question

Oft I find myself wanting to disappear
I try to stay present
I oft find myself not want to die,
But to be nothing, as if dead

There's no signal flare
I seem to want to be sure there's no phoenix rising
I thought that I could just ignore the void
Coming here was supposed to be that, a distraction.

I can run faster when distracted
I know it's still there, still behind me, and yet;
The mountain gives me the strength to run faster.

When I catch my breath it grows closer,
I must run again, I cannot rest

1 year ahead I see nothing,
