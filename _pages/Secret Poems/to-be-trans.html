I was only honest to myself
I needed to be honest with the world
To tell the truth
It's a challenge

To lie is death
Honesty is freeing
Silence is a prison
I stood tall with those I love

I fall down
Brought down to “morality”
Gagged, found, I fall
Into the righteous hall of God, the Lord Devine

I spoke honestly
They slash,They gag,
They bind to The Book
They welcome to the Heaven of God, the Lord Devine

I turned away, chose isolation
Stronger alone, no help
In the lions den I had no one to turn to
Door closed, no escape

Captured
Brought to Him
Sentenced
“You will go to heaven” said they

Down to the Hell of The Lord Devine I fell
Love at His Throne
